## [13:27](https://youtube.com/watch?v=xB30drPllo0&t=807s) Grammar: Genitive 's' (Possession)
In German, an 's' is added to proper names to show possession.

### Grammar Rules:
1. **Standard Names:** Add 's' directly to the end of the name.
    * *Beispiel:* **Kristians** Mutter (Kristian's mother).
2. **Names ending in s, √ü, x, or z:** Add only an apostrophe (**'**), not an extra 's'.
    * *Beispiel:* **Max'** Freundin (Max's girlfriend).
