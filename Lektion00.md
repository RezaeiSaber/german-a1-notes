## Important Letter Combinations [16:53](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1013s)

When letters combine, they often create new sounds.

### Diphthongs (Vowel Combos)
- **ei / ai:** Sounds like "eye".
  - *Example:* **mein** (my), **der Mai** (May) [17:40](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1060s)
- **eu / √§u:** Sounds like "oy" (as in boy).
  - *Example:* **neu** (new), **die B√§ume** (the trees) [18:40](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1120s)
- **ie:** Sounds like a long "ee".
  - *Example:* **die Liebe** (the love) [19:04](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1144s)
- **au:** Sounds like "ow" (as in cow).
  - *Example:* **der Baum** (the tree) [19:18](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1158s)

### Consonant Rules
- **sp / st:** At the beginning of a word, 's' sounds like "sh".
  - *Example:* **Sport** (Shport), **Student** (Shtudent) [20:31](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1231s)
- **s + Vowel:** Sounds like a buzzing 'Z'.
  - *Example:* **die Sonne** (the sun), **super** [21:21](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1281s)
- **sch:** Sounds exactly like "sh".
  - *Example:* **die Schule** (the school) [22:05](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1325s)
- **ch:**
  - After **i, e, √§, √∂, √º** or consonants: Soft palatal sound (Ich-Laut). *Example:* **ich** (I), **nicht** (not) [23:10](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1390s)
  - After **a, o, u, au**: Throat "kh" sound (Ach-Laut). *Example:* **das Buch** (the book), **nach** (after) [23:48](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1428s)
- **tsch:** Sounds like "ch" (as in chat).
  - *Example:* **Deutsch** (German) [24:14](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=1454s)

    - *Example:* **der Vater** (Vah-tah) [09:15](https://youtube.com/watch?v=FxNjYddKnFQ&t=555s)
* **Final -d/-b/-g:** Hardened at the end of words (Auslautverh√§rtung). '-d' sounds like 't', '-b' sounds like 'p', and '-g' sounds like 'k'.
