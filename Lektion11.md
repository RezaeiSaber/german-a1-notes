## Cultural Context
* **Sick Notes (Krankschreibung):** In Germany, if you are ill and cannot work, you must get an official "Arbeitsunf√§higkeitsbescheinigung" (sick note) from a doctor to give to your employer.
* **Health and Sports:** Football is the undisputed #1 sport in Germany. However, health and wellness (Yoga, Pilates, Saunas) are extremely popular in urban centers like Munich.
* **Tea for Recovery:** It is culturally common for German doctors to recommend herbal teas and "leichte Kost" (light food) as the first line of defense for minor illnesses.
