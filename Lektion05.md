## ‚öôÔ∏è Grammar Focus: "Wohin?" and Accusative
[[00:17:45](https://youtu.be/xzNhUFGgtq8?t=1065)] When we talk about movement towards a destination (using verbs like *gehen* or *fahren*), we use the question word **Wohin?** (Where to?) and the **Accusative case**.

### üèóÔ∏è Preposition "In" + Accusative
When moving *into* a place:
1. **Masculine (der):** Changes to **den**.
   - *Der Park* ‚Üí Ich gehe in **den** Park.
   - *Der Club* ‚Üí Ich gehe in **den** Club.
2. **Neuter (das):** Stays **das**, but usually contracts to **ins** (*in + das*).
   - *Das Kino* ‚Üí Ich gehe **ins** Kino.
   - *Das Fitnessstudio* ‚Üí Ich gehe **ins** Fitnessstudio.
3. **Feminine (die):** Stays **die**.
   - *Die Disco* ‚Üí Ich gehe in **die** Disco.
